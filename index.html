Places You MUST Visit

privthi cafe

i visited in may 2019

It is a quaint little restaurant inside a by-lane in Juhu. If you blink you will miss the entrance. Be alert for a black signboard that says "Prithvi Cafe" while you are on the main road. It has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious, and the servings are large. Some dishes can be shared by two people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid dissapointments. On other days the crowd pretty hep & happening. Hosts the amazing, "Prithvi theater fest," which you must go if you can.
